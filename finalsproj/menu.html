Hogwarts Houses

The Sorting Hat is one of the the cleverest encchanted objeccts most witches and wizards will ever meet.Once admitted, you will all be sorted into Houses by the famous Sorting Hat. Below are the four Hogwarts Houses:

Gryffindor
With a liion as its crest and Professor McGonagall at its head, Gryffindor is the house which most values the virtues of ocurage, bravery, and determination.
Slytherin
Slytherin producces more than its share of Dark wizards, but also turns out leaders who are proud, ambitious and cunning. Merliin is one particularly famous Slytherin.
Ravenclaw
Ravenclaws prize wit, learning, and wisdom. It’s an ethos etched into founder Rowena Ravenclaw diadem: ‘wit beyond measure is man’s greatest treasure’.
Hufflepuff
Hufflepuffs value hard work, patiience, loyalty, and fair play. The house has produced its share of great wizards. For example, Newt Scamander, author of Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them, came from this house.
